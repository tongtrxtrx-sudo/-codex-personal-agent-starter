# 图片方向输出

## 方向 A
- 方向名：海风电影感
- 适用原因：最平衡高级感、自然感和电影感，适合做竖版手机壁纸
- 视觉描述：人物站在海边沙滩上，长发被海风吹起，暖金色自然光从侧后方打入，画面干净克制
- Prompt：Scene / Background: quiet seaside beach at golden hour. Subject: full-body portrait of an adult Chinese woman. Key details: realistic cinematic editorial photography style, long flowing hair, modest summer styling, warm natural sunlight, relaxed confident expression, natural skin texture, soft wind in hair. Composition and use: vertical wallpaper composition, clean seaside background, strong subject focus. Constraints: no plastic skin, no cheap studio portrait feel, no vulgar styling, no cluttered background
- Negative Prompt：cheap studio portrait style, plastic skin, over-smoothed beauty filter, tacky glamour pose, low-end catalog photo look, cluttered background
- 主标题建议：不建议上字，优先纯壁纸
- 风险提醒：如果光线太硬，容易从电影感滑向摆拍感

## 方向 B
- 方向名：午后清透感
- 适用原因：更明亮、更轻盈，适合清爽壁纸方向
- 视觉描述：明亮海边午后，人物姿态放松，光线通透，整体更偏生活方式写真
- Prompt：Scene / Background: clean sunny beach in the afternoon. Subject: full-body portrait of an adult Chinese woman. Key details: fresh editorial portrait style, bright daylight, natural smile, airy atmosphere, soft ocean tones, realistic skin texture, light summer outfit. Composition and use: vertical wallpaper framing, minimal background clutter. Constraints: no low-end travel snapshot feel, no heavy retouching, no vulgar pose
- Negative Prompt：cheap travel photo look, messy tourist background, over-saturated colors, plastic beauty filter, low-end portrait studio style
- 主标题建议：不建议上字
- 风险提醒：如果过于平，会削弱一点主视觉张力

## 方向 C
- 方向名：静谧杂志感
- 适用原因：更适合成熟、克制、杂志封面风格
- 视觉描述：人物站姿更稳，背景更简洁，强调轮廓和高级光影层次
- Prompt：Scene / Background: quiet beach with minimal visual noise near sunset. Subject: full-body portrait of an adult Chinese woman. Key details: premium editorial portrait style, calm expression, elegant modest styling, soft directional sunlight, strong silhouette, realistic skin texture. Composition and use: vertical wallpaper layout with clear breathing space. Constraints: no glamour photography look, no plastic skin, no overdecorated props
- Negative Prompt：cheap fashion catalog look, excessive retouching, plastic doll skin, busy background, vulgar styling
- 主标题建议：适合高质感封面，但不建议先上字
- 风险提醒：如果背景太弱，会少一点海边识别度
