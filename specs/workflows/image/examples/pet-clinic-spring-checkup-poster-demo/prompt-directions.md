# 图片方向输出

## 方向 A
- 方向名：临床信任感
- 适用原因：最适合强调专业与安心，适合作为门店海报主视觉
- 视觉描述：明亮整洁的宠物诊室中，医生正在为狗做基础检查，猫在主人怀里，画面留有清晰信息区
- Prompt：clean veterinary spring checkup campaign poster, bright clinic interior, veterinarian checking a friendly dog, cat held by owner, warm and trustworthy mood, white light green light blue palette, realistic modern pet healthcare branding, clean composition, strong readable information area for headline price and deadline
- Negative Prompt：cartoon pets, cheap sales flyer style, crowded layout, neon palette, horror medical feeling, exaggerated sick-animal emotion, noisy retail promotion look
- 主标题建议：春季宠物体检周
- 风险提醒：如果诊室器械过强，可能会让画面偏冷

## 方向 B
- 方向名：温柔陪伴感
- 适用原因：更适合突出宠物主和医院之间的信任关系
- 视觉描述：宠物主陪同猫狗做检查，医生微笑沟通，整体更生活化、更有陪伴感
- Prompt：warm pet clinic spring health campaign poster, pet owner with cat and dog during gentle checkup, friendly veterinarian interaction, soft daylight, clean lifestyle composition, reassuring and caring tone, subtle green blue palette, reserved space for headline price and deadline
- Negative Prompt：overly cute cartoon style, clutter, hard-sale retail poster look, dramatic medical equipment, exaggerated expressions, messy information area
- 主标题建议：给毛孩子做个春季检查
- 风险提醒：如果生活化太强，可能削弱医疗专业感

## 方向 C
- 方向名：城市品质感
- 适用原因：更适合年轻宠物主审美，适合朋友圈传播
- 视觉描述：现代感宠物医疗空间中，年轻宠物主带宠物做春季健康检查，画面更轻、更时尚
- Prompt：modern urban pet wellness poster, young pet owners bringing cat and dog for spring health check, premium clean clinic style, airy composition, contemporary branding, soft green and blue accents, elegant but practical information area for price and deadline
- Negative Prompt：luxury fashion ad look, cartoon illustration, discount promo poster, overly packed layout, noisy background, sterile hospital coldness
- 主标题建议：春季检查 别拖到不舒服
- 风险提醒：如果画面过于时尚，可能会削弱体检活动信息感
