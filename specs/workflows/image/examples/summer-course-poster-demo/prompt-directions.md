# 图片方向输出

## 方向 A
- 方向名：清新教育感
- 适用原因：最适合强调家长信任感和课堂氛围
- 视觉描述：明亮教室环境中，老师与孩子小班互动，画面整洁，蓝白黄为主
- Prompt：a bright educational summer English class poster, small-group classroom interaction, children speaking confidently, clean composition, blue white yellow palette, trustworthy and warm mood, realistic modern education branding, clear information area for headline and deadline
- Negative Prompt：cheap promotion style, excessive cartoon, cluttered layout, neon colors, crowded poster elements, low-end flyer look
- 主标题建议：9.9元暑期体验课
- 风险提醒：如果老师形象过强，可能削弱孩子开口互动感

## 方向 B
- 方向名：暑期活力感
- 适用原因：更适合突出暑期氛围和体验感，但仍保持教育感
- 视觉描述：孩子在轻松明亮的暑期课堂中互动练习，有少量夏日元素点缀
- Prompt：summer English trial class poster for kids, bright lively classroom, children interacting and speaking, subtle summer accents, friendly educational tone, light blue and yellow, modern composition, space reserved for headline and signup deadline
- Negative Prompt：overly festive summer beach style, excessive cartoon, messy composition, discount-store promotion look, childish cheap design
- 主标题建议：暑假让孩子敢开口
- 风险提醒：夏日元素过多会把画面拉向活动海报而不是教育海报

## 方向 C
- 方向名：家长信任感
- 适用原因：更适合强调专业、稳重和报名决策感
- 视觉描述：更干净稳重的机构海报风格，重点突出小班教学和信息清晰
- Prompt：professional kids English trial course poster, small class teaching scene, trustworthy education brand tone, clean structured layout, parents decision-oriented poster style, blue white palette, clear hierarchy, strong information area
- Negative Prompt：overly playful illustration, cheap sales poster, overdecorated background, crowded text space, exaggerated expressions
- 主标题建议：小班英语体验课报名中
- 风险提醒：如果画面太稳重，可能会削弱暑期体验的轻松感
